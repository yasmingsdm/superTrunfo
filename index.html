Super Trunfo

How to Play:

  1. Look at your card and choose the attribute you want to compete with;
  2. If your card has a higher number on the attribute selected than the computer's card, you win!
    If the computer's card has a higher number, you loose!
    If it is the same, it's a tie!
  3. You will play with 6 different cards before the game is over.
